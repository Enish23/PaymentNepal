About Us

Payment Nepal is a financial technology startup company that is authorised by Nepal Rastra Bank (The Central Bank). Our first mission is to create a payment platform through internet. The e-commerce platform is used by Small Medium Enterprises (SME) & other individuals/groups of Nepal having a potential to sell their services or products to the audience (customer) in Nepal & abroad. They can accept payments & manage their businesses online.

Our sole ambition is to create a bridge between people & technology in order to create a more cashless economy & increase the foreign currency earning of Nepal through a cross border e-commerce. We endeavor to make the internet a soul of our economy. Our ambition could be a metaphor of positive upheaval in the way we do the local & cross border business. We want to bring all entrepreneurs & other parties from every nook & corner of the country under e-commerce umbrella.
